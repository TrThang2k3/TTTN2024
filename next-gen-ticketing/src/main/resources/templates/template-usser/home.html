Next-Gen Ticket Bus

Welcome to Next-Gen Ticket Bus Solution! We are proud to introduce an advanced bus ticket system, designed to improve the passenger experience and optimize the operations of bus service operators.

Our system connects to the Ticketer Smart Gateway to communicate with vehicles, creating a connected ecosystem for IT infrastructure on buses. For electric vehicles, it also enables advanced tracking of energy consumption. The system provides real-time information to passengers through journey assistance applications and displays at transfer stations. This improves the passenger experience and optimizes operator operational management. Our ticket machines are integrated with a separate card reader, which gives us more information about our passengers' origin and destination. This simultaneously enhances the management and monitoring capabilities of the system.
